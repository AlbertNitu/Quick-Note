We're so honored that you have chosen Quick Note! Let us introduce you to our service:
Quick Note is a tool that allows you to jot down your thoughts or plans by creating notes or tasks that you can view later.

Notes can store a large amount of content, (but don't exaggerate!) allowing you to add a title and content. They are ideal for making a list of your favorite songs, recipes, or even taking online notes.
On the other hand, tasks allow you to store a goal, to-do, or anything else that is one line. You can also set a due date for your tasks.

You can easily add tasks and notes by pressing the plus button at the top right corner of the notes or tasks page, and retrieve your notes and tasks anytime on any computer. Please note that your notes and tasks will only be available if you are signed in with your Google Account. If you aren't, your notes and tasks will be saved locally.

So now that you've had an introduction on how Quick Note works, continue to see its amazing functionalities!
Enjoy!
